Trash Thrash

Role: Gameplay Designer, Illustrator
Tools: Procreate, Photoshop, TinkerCAD

Trash Thrash is a dicebuilding board game where players employ trash animals to battle their pesky neighbor. Players can build their home using 3 cards, a lawn, porch, and house, then draft dice from a shared dice pool. The first player to defeat their neighbor's animals and destroy their home wins the game.

This game was made for the Fall 2021 course, Game Design Methodologies, at Rutgers University.
