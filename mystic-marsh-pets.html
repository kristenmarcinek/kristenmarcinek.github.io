Mystic Marsh Pets

Role: Character Designer, Illustrator, 3D Artist
Tools: Procreate, Photoshop, Maya

Mystic Marsh Pets is an upcoming blind box toy line featuring magical marshy creatures. It is an amalgamation of many things, but is mostly inspired by my deep love and advocacy for the unique critters that are not traditionally represented in toys or entertainment.
